## 9. India Regulatory Acknowledgment

Users accessing the Service from India should be aware of the following regulatory framework:

- Cryptocurrencies are classified as **Virtual Digital Assets (VDAs)** under Section 2(47A) of the Income Tax Act, 1961, as amended by the Finance Act, 2022.
- Gains from the transfer of VDAs are subject to a **30% income tax** (plus applicable surcharge and cess) under Section 115BBH, with no deductions permitted other than the cost of acquisition.
- A **1% Tax Deducted at Source (TDS)** applies to the transfer of VDAs under Section 194S, where the consideration exceeds the prescribed threshold.
- Losses from VDA transactions cannot be set off against income from any other source and cannot be carried forward.
- The regulatory landscape for VDAs in India is evolving. Users are responsible for staying informed about and complying with all applicable laws, regulations, and tax obligations.

Delta Saraswati does not provide tax advice. Consult a qualified tax professional for guidance on your specific obligations.
